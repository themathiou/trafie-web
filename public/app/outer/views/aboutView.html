About

What is Trafie?

Trafie is a free web and iOS application that helps you, the Track and Field athlete, create your personal profile, store your achievements from the competitions you have entered, and then analyze your performance data, in an easy and intuitive way.

Also, Trafie is a social tool, allowing you to share your victories with the world, or find other athletes you may know.

How can Trafie help me?

By recording your competitions and your performance, you can see your progress in time, keep statistics, set new goals, and remember your best performances. Also you can take notes like the direction and speed of the wind during a competition, injuries etc.

All the recorded competitions can be filtered, so it is easy for example to see all the competitions you entered in 2015 of a certain discipline.

Track and field is about great victories, which become more important when they are shared with others. Trafie makes it easy for you to share your best or latest competitions, and show that photo of yours with the hard-earned gold medal!
